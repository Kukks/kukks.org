Freedom tech for an unfree world

My name is Andrew Camilleri, and usually go by the online handle Kukks.

I am a software consultant with a focus on dotnet and Bitcoin. My passion is to build solutions that help empower others to reclaim their sovereignty, in a world that moves to eradicate it. Practically everything I build is free, open source, and openly licensed.

Almost all my work revolves around Bitcoin. It's simply the greatest technology that I can work on to provide the most meaningful impact. As the world around us tightens its grip on our freedoms, Bitcoin provides us a way to reclaim our financial freedom and fight back.
